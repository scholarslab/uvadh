Mission

SHANTI strives to promote a culture of innovation and excellence in humanities, sciences and arts within the academic life of the University of Virginia. SHANTI believes information technology offers uniquely powerful tools to facilitate such innovation and excellence in research, learning, publication, and community engagement. To that end, SHANTI supports a digital ecology.

Activities

  • A suite of interconnected digital applications supporting the creation and dissemination of scholarly content on the Web;
  • User-driven documentation on a variety of tools and platforms, written by and for the UVA community;
  • Consultations and support for using SHANTI-supported tools in teaching and research.
