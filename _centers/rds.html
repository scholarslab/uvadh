Mission

Research Data Services includes several teams focused on collecting, managing, analyzing, and preserving research data. They provide training and consultations on data analysis and statistical methods, offer support for research software, and assist in discovering, acquiring data and managing your data.

Activities

  • Consultations and training on statistical methods and data analysis;
  • Accessing and installing UVA site-licensed software;
  • Developing effective search strategies for finding data;
  • Accessing and using data sources in the library;
  • Strategies for collecting, documenting, and organizing data;
  • Drafting and reviewing data management plans;
